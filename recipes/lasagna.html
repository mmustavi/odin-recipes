Lasagna

Description

Delight your taste buds with our scrumptious lasagna, a classic Italian dish that combines layers of pasta, rich meaty sauce, and indulgent cheese. Baked to perfection, our lasagna is a comforting and satisfying meal that will leave you craving for more!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles: 12 sheets
  • Ground beef: 1 pound
  • Italian sausage: 1/2 pound
  • Onion: 1 large, finely chopped
  • Garlic cloves: 3, minced
  • Tomato sauce: 2 cups
  • Tomato paste: 1/4 cup
  • Crushed tomatoes: 1 can (14 ounces)
  • Fresh basil leaves: 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Dried oregano: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Black pepper: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Ricotta cheese: 2 cups
  • Mozzarella cheese: 2 cups, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese: 1/2 cup, grated
  • Fresh parsley leaves: 2 tablespoons, chopped
  • Olive oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Egg: 1, lightly beaten

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, cook until softened.
  3. Add ground beef and Italian sausage to the skillet. Cook until browned and no longer pink.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese, beaten egg, and parsley
  6. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  9. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce and cheese on top.
  11. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  13. Let the lasagna rest for a few minutes before serving to allow it to set.
  14. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
