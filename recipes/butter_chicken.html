Butter Chicken

Description

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a classic and popular Indian dish. It features tender pieces of chicken cooked in a creamy and flavorful tomato-based sauce. This quick and easy recipe will satisfy your taste buds with its rich and aromatic flavors.

Ingredients

    500 grams boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces

    2 tablespoons butter

    1 onion, finely chopped

    2 cloves garlic, minced

    1 teaspoon ginger, grated

    1 teaspoon ground cumin

    1 teaspoon ground coriander

    1 teaspoon paprika

    1/2 teaspoon turmeric

    1/2 teaspoon garam masala

    1 cup tomato puree

    1/2 cup heavy cream

    Salt, to taste

    Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Steps

    In a large skillet or pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and cook until they become translucent.

    Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pan. Saut√© for a minute until fragrant.

    Now, add the chicken pieces to the pan and cook until they are lightly browned on all sides.

    In a small bowl, mix together the ground cumin, ground coriander, paprika, turmeric, and garam masala. Sprinkle this spice mixture over the chicken, stirring well to coat evenly.

    Pour in the tomato puree and stir to combine. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.

    Stir in the heavy cream and season with salt according to your taste. Allow the sauce to simmer for an additional 5 minutes to thicken slightly.

    Remove the pan from the heat. Garnish the butter chicken with fresh cilantro leaves.

    Serve the butter chicken hot with steamed rice, naan bread, or roti. Enjoy this delicious and creamy Indian dish!
